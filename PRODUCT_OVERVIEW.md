# Jira Intelligence Platform

**Transform project management from reactive data analysis to proactive decision automation.**

---

## Executive Summary

This platform turns raw Jira data into actionable intelligence. Instead of drowning in dashboards, managers get answers to one question: **"What should I do right now?"**

**Core Value Proposition:**
- Save **3-6 hours per manager per week**
- Prevent problems **before** they become crises
- Eliminate decision fatigue with AI-powered recommendations
